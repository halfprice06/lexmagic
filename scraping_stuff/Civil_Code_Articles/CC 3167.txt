Art. 3167.  Pledgee not bound for pledgor's obligations
            In the absence of an assumption by the pledgee, the existence of a pledge does not
impose upon the pledgee liability for the pledgor's acts or omissions, nor does it bind the
pledgee to perform the pledgor's obligations.
            Acts 2014, No. 281, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
