Art. 3337.  Cancellation of mortgages and privileges from the records
The recorder shall cancel a mortgage or privilege from his records in the manner prescribed by law.
Acts 1992, No. 1132, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1993; Acts 2005, No. 169, §2, eff. July 1, 2006; Acts 2005, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 13, §1, eff. Nov. 29, 2005.
