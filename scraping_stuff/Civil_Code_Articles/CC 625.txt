Art. 625.  Intervention by creditors of the usufructuary
A creditor of the usufructuary may intervene and may prevent termination of the usufruct and delivery of the property to the naked owner by offering to repair the damages caused by the usufructuary and by giving security for the future.
Acts 1976, No. 103, §1; Acts 2010, No. 881, §1, eff. July 2, 2010.
