Art. 3524.  Immovables situated in this state 
Except as otherwise provided in this Title, the rights and obligations of spouses with regard to immovables situated in this state are governed by the law of this state.  Whether such immovables are community or separate property is determined in accordance with the law of this state, regardless of the domicile of the acquiring spouse at the time of acquisition.
Acts 1991, No. 923, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
