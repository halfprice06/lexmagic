Art. 89.  Impediment of same sex
           Persons of the same sex may not contract marriage with each other.  A
purported marriage between persons of the same sex contracted in another
state shall be governed by the provisions of Title II of Book IV of the Civil
Code.
           Acts 1987, No. 886, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1988; Acts 1999, No. 890, §1.
