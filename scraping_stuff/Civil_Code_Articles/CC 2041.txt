Art. 2041.  Action must be brought within one year
            The action of the obligee must be brought within one year from the time he learned
or should have learned of the act, or the result of the failure to act, of the obligor that the
obligee seeks to annul, but never after three years from the date of that act or result.
            Acts 1984, No. 331, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985; Acts 2013, No. 88, §1; Acts 2021, No. 414,
§1.
