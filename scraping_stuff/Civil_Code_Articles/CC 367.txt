Art. 367.  Emancipation by marriage
            A minor sixteen or seventeen years of age is fully emancipated by marriage.
Termination of the marriage does not affect emancipation by marriage. Emancipation by
marriage may not be modified or terminated.
            Acts 2008, No. 786, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2019, No. 401, §1.
