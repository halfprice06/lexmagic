CHAPTER 5.   PARENTAL AUTHORITY OF MARRIED PERSONS
SECTION 1.  GENERAL PRINCIPLES OF PARENTAL AUTHORITY
Art. 221.  Authority of married parents
            The father and mother who are married to each other have parental authority over
their minor child during the marriage.
            Amended by Acts 1916, No. 41; Acts 1920, No. 252; Acts 1924, No. 197; Acts 2015,
No. 260, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
