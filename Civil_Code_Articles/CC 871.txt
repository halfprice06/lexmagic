TITLE I--OF SUCCESSIONS
CHAPTER 1--OF THE DIFFERENT SORTS OF
SUCCESSIONS AND SUCCESSORS 
Art. 871.  Meaning of succession
           Succession is the transmission of the estate of the deceased to his
successors.  The successors thus have the right to take possession of the estate
of the deceased after complying with applicable provisions of law.
           Acts 1981 No. 919, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
