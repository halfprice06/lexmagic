Art. 360.  Parents' rights of administration
           In addition to the rights of tutorship, the parents shall retain, during the
marriage and for the minority of the child with an intellectual disability, all rights of
administration granted to parents of children without an intellectual disability during
their minority.
           Acts 1966, No. 496, §2; Acts 2014, No. 811, §30, eff. June 23, 2014.
