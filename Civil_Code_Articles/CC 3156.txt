Art. 3156.  Pledgee's right of retention
            If the thing pledged has been delivered to the pledgee or a third person for the benefit
of the pledgee, the pledgee is not obligated to return it until all secured obligations have been
extinguished.
            Amended by Acts 1981, No. 315, §1; Acts 2014, No. 281, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
