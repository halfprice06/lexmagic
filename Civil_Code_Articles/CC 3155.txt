Art. 3155.  Effectiveness against third persons of the pledge of other obligations
            If the thing pledged is another person's obligation not arising under the lease of an
immovable, the pledge is effective against third persons only from the time that the obligor
has actual knowledge of the pledge or has been given notice of it.
            Acts 2014, No. 281, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
